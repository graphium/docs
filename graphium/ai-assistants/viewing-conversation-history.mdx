## Overview

Your AI conversation history is accessible in three ways: recent conversations appear as cards on the vault **Home** page, the **CHATS** quick-access list in the vault sidebar shows the same recent conversations alongside the rest of the vault navigation, and the full time-grouped history is available via the clock icon in the top-right corner of the page.

## When to Use This

Use this when you want to revisit a previous analysis, continue an earlier conversation, or review an AI response from a past session.

## Prerequisites

- You must have at least one previous conversation with the AI Assistant in this vault

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Viewing Recent Chats from the Home Page

1. Open a vault. The **Home** page displays automatically.
2. The **RECENT CHATS** section shows your four most recent conversations as cards.
3. Each card shows the conversation title, the assistant used (e.g., *Data Analyst*, *Operations Helper*), and a relative timestamp (e.g., *5m ago*, *Yesterday*).
4. Click any card to reopen that conversation.

### Viewing Recent Chats from the Sidebar

1. In the vault sidebar, find the **CHATS** section.
2. The most recent conversations are listed; click **[N] More** to expand the list.
3. Click any conversation title to reopen it.

### Viewing the Full Chat History

1. On the vault Home page, click the **clock icon** in the top-right corner.
2. The **Chat History** panel opens, showing all past conversations grouped by time:
   - **TODAY**
   - **YESTERDAY**
   - **PREVIOUS 7 DAYS**
   - …and older groupings as needed.
3. Each entry shows the conversation title, the assistant used, and a relative timestamp.
4. Click any conversation to reopen it.

### Starting a New Conversation

You have a few options for starting a fresh conversation:

- Click the chat input bar on the Home page and type your question.
- Click the **New Chat** button at the top of the Chat History panel.
- Click an assistant card in the **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** section on the Home page to start a conversation with that assistant.

New conversations default to the **General** assistant unless you pick a specialized assistant first.

## What to Expect

Reopening a conversation allows you to continue asking follow-up questions — the AI retains the context of the previous messages.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| No recent chats appear on Home | No conversations have been started yet | Begin a new conversation by typing in the chat input bar |
| A past conversation is not in Recent Chats | It may be older than the 4 most recent | Click the clock icon to access the full time-grouped history |
| CHATS section in the sidebar is empty | No conversations have been started in this vault | Start a new conversation from the Home page |
