## Overview

When you open a vault in Graphium AI, the **Home** page is your AI-powered starting point. It displays your recent conversations, suggested prompts, the assistants available for this vault, and a chat input bar — giving you immediate access to ask questions about your vault data without navigating elsewhere.

## When to Use This

Use this as your starting point whenever you want to ask questions about your vault data, request summaries, or explore insights using the AI.

## Prerequisites

- You must be a member of the vault with at least Contributor access
- The vault must have at least one AI Assistant configured

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open the Vault

From the workspace dashboard, click on any vault to open it. The **Home** page loads automatically as the default view.

### Step 2: Review the Home Page

The Home page displays:

- A **chat input bar** at the top with the placeholder *"Ask a question about your data..."*
- A **RECENT CHATS** section showing your four most recent conversations as cards — each showing the conversation title, which assistant was used, and how long ago it was
- A **TRY ASKING** section with suggested prompts relevant to your vault data
- An **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** section listing the assistants configured for this vault (e.g., *Data Analyst*, *Operations Helper*, *Compliance Monitor*). Click an assistant card to start a new conversation with it.

### Step 3: Review the Vault Sidebar

The vault sidebar, to the left of the Home page, gives you quick access to everything in the vault:

- **Home** — the AI Assistant welcome screen
- **Tasks** — task list for the vault (with a count badge for open items)
- **Connections** — automated connections to external systems
- **Assistants** — list of AI assistants configured for this vault
- **Playbooks** — automated playbooks for the vault
- **Settings** — vault settings
- **TABLES** — a quick-access list of tables in the vault
- **CHATS** — a quick-access list of recent conversations

A **Builder View** / **Contributor View** toggle at the bottom of the sidebar lets you switch between roles you have access to. The **Collapse sidebar** button hides the sidebar to give the main page more room.

### Step 4: Start a New Conversation

Click the chat input bar and type your question, click one of the **TRY ASKING** suggestions to start with a pre-built prompt, or click an assistant card in **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** to start a conversation with a specific assistant.

### Step 5: Return to a Recent Chat

Click any card in the **RECENT CHATS** section to reopen a previous conversation. To browse older conversations, click the **clock icon** in the top-right corner to open the full chat history.

## What to Expect

After typing a message and sending it, the conversation view opens with your question and the AI's response. The Home page is always accessible by clicking **Home** in the vault sidebar.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| No recent chats appear | No previous conversations exist for this vault | Start a new conversation by typing in the chat input bar |
| AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS section is empty | No assistants are configured for this vault | Open the **Assistants** section in the sidebar and configure one |
| Home is not visible in the sidebar | The vault may not have the AI feature enabled | Contact your vault Admin |
