## Overview

When you open a vault in Graphium AI, the **Home** page is your AI-powered starting point. It displays your recent conversations, suggested prompts, the assistants available for this vault, and a chat input bar — giving you immediate access to ask questions about your vault data. The Home page also provides access to your full conversation history across past sessions.

---

## When to Use This

Use the Home page as your starting point whenever you want to ask questions about your vault data, request summaries, explore insights using AI, or return to a previous conversation.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must be a member of the vault with at least Contributor access
- The vault must have at least one AI Assistant configured

---

## Opening the Vault

From the workspace dashboard, click on any vault to open it. The **Home** page loads automatically as the default view.

---

## The Home Page

The Home page displays:

- A **chat input bar** at the top with the placeholder *"Ask a question about your data..."*
- A **RECENT CHATS** section showing your four most recent conversations as cards — each showing the conversation title, which assistant was used, and how long ago it was
- A **TRY ASKING** section with suggested prompts relevant to your vault data
- An **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** section listing the assistants configured for this vault (e.g., *Data Analyst*, *Operations Helper*, *Compliance Monitor*). Click an assistant card to start a new conversation with it.

---

## The Vault Sidebar

The vault sidebar, to the left of the Home page, gives you quick access to everything in the vault:

- **Home** — the AI Assistant home page
- **Tasks** — task list for the vault (with a count badge for open items)
- **Connections** — automated connections to external systems
- **Assistants** — list of AI assistants configured for this vault
- **Playbooks** — automated playbooks for the vault
- **Settings** — vault settings
- **TABLES** — a quick-access list of tables in the vault
- **RECENT CHATS** — a quick-access list of recent conversations

A **Builder View** / **Contributor View** toggle at the bottom of the sidebar lets you switch between roles you have access to. The **Collapse sidebar** button hides the sidebar to give the main page more room.

---

## Starting a New Conversation

You have a few options for starting a fresh conversation:

- Click the **chat input bar** and type your question
- Click one of the **TRY ASKING** suggestions to start with a pre-built prompt
- Click an assistant card in **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** to start a conversation with a specific assistant

New conversations default to the **General** assistant unless you pick a specialized assistant first.

---

## Accessing Conversation History

Your conversation history is accessible in three ways.

**From the Home page**

The **RECENT CHATS** section shows your four most recent conversations as cards. Each card shows the conversation title, the assistant used, and a relative timestamp (e.g., *5m ago*, *Yesterday*). Click any card to reopen that conversation.

**From the sidebar**

In the vault sidebar, the **RECENT CHATS** section lists your most recent conversations. Click **[N] More** to expand the list. Click any conversation title to reopen it.

**From the full history panel**

Click the **clock icon** in the top-right corner of the Home page to open the full **Chat History** panel. Conversations are grouped by time — **TODAY**, **YESTERDAY**, **PREVIOUS 7 DAYS**, and older groupings as needed. Each entry shows the conversation title, the assistant used, and a relative timestamp. Click any conversation to reopen it, or click **New Chat** at the top of the panel to start a fresh conversation.

---

## What to Expect

Reopening a conversation allows you to continue asking follow-up questions — the AI retains the context of the previous messages. The Home page is always accessible by clicking **Home** in the vault sidebar.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| No recent chats appear on Home | No conversations have been started yet | Start a new conversation by typing in the chat input bar |
| AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS section is empty | No assistants are configured for this vault | Open the **Assistants** section in the sidebar and configure one |
| Home is not visible in the sidebar | The vault may not have the AI feature enabled | Contact your vault Admin |
| A past conversation is not in Recent Chats | It may be older than the 4 most recent | Click the clock icon to access the full time-grouped history |
| CHATS section in the sidebar is empty | No conversations have been started in this vault | Start a new conversation from the Home page |
