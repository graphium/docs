## Overview

Every conversation you have with the AI Assistant is saved and accessible from the conversation history panel on the left side of the chat interface. Conversations are grouped by recency so you can quickly find and reopen a previous chat.

## When to Use This

Use this when you want to revisit a previous analysis, continue an earlier conversation, or review an AI response from a past session.

## Prerequisites

- You must have at least one previous conversation with the AI Assistant in this vault

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open AI Assistant

In the vault sidebar, click **AI Assistant**. The conversation history panel appears on the left side of the screen.

### Step 2: Browse the History

Conversations are grouped into three time periods:

| Group | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Today** | Conversations started today |
| **Yesterday** | Conversations started yesterday |
| **Previous 7 Days** | Conversations from the past week |

Each conversation entry shows:
- A snippet of the opening message
- The name of the assistant used (e.g., *Data Analyst*, *Operations Helper*)
- A relative timestamp (e.g., *5m ago*, *2h ago*, *Yesterday*, *3d ago*)

### Step 3: Reopen a Conversation

Click any conversation in the history panel to reopen it. The full conversation — including all messages and responses — loads in the main chat area.

### Step 4: Start a New Conversation

Click **+ New Chat** at the top of the history panel to start a fresh conversation without affecting any existing ones.

### Step 5: Collapse the History Panel

Click the **←** arrow at the top-right of the history panel to collapse it and give more space to the chat area. Click the arrow again to expand it.

## What to Expect

Conversation history is stored per vault and is visible to you based on your own sessions. Reopening a conversation allows you to continue asking follow-up questions — the AI retains the context of the previous messages.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| A conversation is not visible in the history | It may be older than 7 days or was cleared | Scroll down in the history panel to check; older conversations may not be shown |
| History panel is not visible | It may have been collapsed | Click the arrow icon on the left edge of the chat area to expand it |
