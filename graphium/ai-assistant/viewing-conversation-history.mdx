## Overview

Your AI conversation history is accessible in two ways: recent conversations appear as cards on the vault **Home** page, and the full history is available via the clock icon in the top-right corner of the page.

## When to Use This

Use this when you want to revisit a previous analysis, continue an earlier conversation, or review an AI response from a past session.

## Prerequisites

- You must have at least one previous conversation with the AI Assistant in this vault

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Viewing Recent Chats

1. Open a vault. The **Home** page displays automatically.
2. The **RECENT CHATS** section shows your four most recent conversations as cards in a 2×2 grid.
3. Each card shows the conversation title, the assistant used (e.g., *Data Analyst*, *Operations Helper*), and a relative timestamp (e.g., *5m ago*, *Yesterday*).
4. Click any card to reopen that conversation.

### Viewing Full History

1. On the vault Home page, click the **clock icon** (🕐) in the top-right corner.
2. The full conversation history opens, showing all past conversations for this vault.
3. Click any conversation to reopen it.

### Starting a New Conversation

To start a fresh conversation, click the chat input bar on the Home page and type your question. A new conversation is created automatically.

## What to Expect

Reopening a conversation allows you to continue asking follow-up questions — the AI retains the context of the previous messages.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| No recent chats appear on Home | No conversations have been started yet | Begin a new conversation by typing in the chat input bar |
| A past conversation is not in Recent Chats | It may be older than the 4 most recent | Click the clock icon to access the full history |
