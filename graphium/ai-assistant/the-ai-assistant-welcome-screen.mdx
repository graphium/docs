## Overview

When you open the AI Assistant for the first time in a conversation — or start a new chat — you are greeted with the AI Assistant welcome screen. This screen gives you a quick overview of what the assistant can do, shows which assistants are available in the vault, and surfaces suggested prompts to help you get started quickly.

## When to Use This

Use this as your starting point whenever you want to ask questions about your vault data, request summaries, or explore insights using the AI.

## Prerequisites

- You must be a member of the vault with at least Contributor access
- The vault must have at least one AI Assistant configured

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open AI Assistant

In the vault sidebar, click **AI Assistant**. If no active conversation is selected, the welcome screen appears.

### Step 2: Review What the Assistant Can Do

The welcome screen displays:

- A title: **"AI Assistant Ready"**
- A description: *"Ask questions about your data, request summaries, or explore insights. Use the ⚙ button to configure which tables, tools, and connections the AI can access."*

### Step 3: Try a Suggested Prompt

The **TRY ASKING** section displays example prompts relevant to your vault data — such as *"Analyze turnover times"* or *"Provider performance"*. Click any suggestion to use it as your first message.

### Step 4: Choose an Assistant

The **AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS** section shows the configured AI assistants for this vault. Each assistant has a different focus and data access level. Click an assistant card to start a conversation with it, or use the chat bubble icon in the input bar to switch assistants.

### Step 5: Start a New Chat Anytime

Click **+ New Chat** at the top of the conversation history panel to return to the welcome screen and start a fresh conversation.

## What to Expect

After selecting an assistant and sending your first message, the chat interface opens with your question and the AI's response. The welcome screen is replaced by the active conversation view.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| No assistants appear in the AVAILABLE ASSISTANTS section | No AI assistants have been configured for this vault | Contact your vault Admin to set up an AI Assistant |
| AI Assistant is not visible in the sidebar | Your vault role may not include AI Assistant access | Contact your vault Admin |
