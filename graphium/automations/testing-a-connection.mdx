## Overview

The Test button in the connection detail view runs a real-time health check against the external system to verify that the connection is active and authentication is valid.

## When to Use This

Use this after setting up a new connection, after updating authentication credentials, or when you suspect a connection may have stopped working.

## Prerequisites

- Admin or Builder vault role
- A connection configured in the vault

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open the Connection Detail View

Navigate to **Automations → Connections**, then click on a connection to open its detail view.

### Step 2: Click Test

Click the **Test** button in the top-right header of the connection detail view. Graphium sends a health check request to the external system.

### Step 3: Review the Result

The result displays inline. A successful test confirms the connection is active and credentials are valid. A failed test indicates a connectivity or authentication issue.

## What to Expect

After clicking Test, a **log** event appears in the Activity Log confirming whether the health check passed or failed.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Test fails immediately | Authentication credentials are invalid or expired | Update credentials in the Authentication section and test again |
| Test times out | External system is unreachable | Check that the external system is online and accessible |
