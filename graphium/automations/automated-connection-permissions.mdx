## Overview

Access to the Connections section and the ability to manage connections is controlled by vault roles. Understanding which roles can perform which actions helps Workspace Owners configure the right access for their team members.

## Permissions by Role

| Action | Admin | Builder | Contributor | Viewer |
|--------|-------|---------|-------------|--------|
| View the Connections page | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| View connection details | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Add a new connection | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Enable or disable a connection | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Edit connection configuration | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Test a connection | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| View the activity log | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |

Contributor and Viewer roles do not have access to the Automations section and cannot view or manage connections.
