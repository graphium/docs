## Overview

The Connections section in Automations gives you a complete view of all data connections configured for a vault. Each connection links Graphium to an external system — such as an EMR, file server, or spreadsheet — and allows data to flow in and out automatically. Connections are accessible to users with an Admin or Builder vault role.

## When to Use This

Use this when you need to see which external systems are connected to your vault, check the status or last activity of a connection, or navigate to a connection's detail view.

## Prerequisites

- You must be a member of the vault with an Admin or Builder role
- The vault must have at least one connection configured

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open the Vault

From the workspace dashboard, click the vault you want to view.

### Step 2: Navigate to Automations

In the left sidebar, click **Automations**. The sidebar expands to show **Connections** and **Workflows**.

### Step 3: Click Connections

Click **Connections** in the Automations sub-navigation. The **Data Connections** page opens showing all configured connections and a count (e.g., *"6 connections"*).

If no connections have been added, the page displays: *"No connections yet — Connect to external databases or APIs to sync data with this vault."*

## Switching Between Card and List View

Use the view toggle in the top-right corner of the Data Connections page:

- **Grid icon (⊞)** — card view (default): displays each connection as a card showing its icon, name, type, status badge, last activity, and enabled/disabled indicator
- **List icon (☰)** — list view: displays connections in a table with columns for CONNECTION, TYPE, STATUS, LAST ACTIVITY, and ENABLED

Click the **">"** chevron at the end of any list row — or anywhere on a card — to open the connection's detail view.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Connections page is not visible | Your vault role is Contributor or Viewer | Contact your vault Admin |
| A connection you expected is not listed | It may have been removed or was never added | Check with your Admin or add it via + Add Connection |
