## Overview

The bell icon in the global navigation bar is your notification center. It shows how many unread notifications you have across the current workspace and gives you a single place to act on them — reading, navigating to the relevant resource, or dismissing alerts you no longer need.

---

## The Bell Icon and Unread Count

The bell icon appears in the top navigation bar, visible from anywhere in the application. When you have unread notifications in the current workspace, a badge on the icon displays the unread count. The count reflects only unread notifications — notifications you have already read are not included in the badge, even if you have not dismissed them.

---

## Opening the Notification Dropdown

Click the bell icon to open the notification dropdown. The dropdown displays your **active notifications** — these are all notifications that are either unread or read but not yet dismissed. Notifications you have dismissed do not appear.

Each notification shows what event occurred, which vault or workspace it relates to, and when it was generated. Unread notifications are visually distinguished from read ones.

If there are no active notifications, the dropdown shows an empty state indicating your notification feed is clear.

---

## Acting on Notifications

**Mark one as read**

Click the read action on an individual notification to mark it as read. This removes the unread indicator and reduces the badge count, but the notification remains in your dropdown as an active notification until you dismiss it.

**Mark all as read**

Use the "Mark all as read" option to mark every unread notification in the dropdown as read at once. This clears the badge count without removing any notifications from your feed.

**Dismiss one notification**

Click the dismiss action on an individual notification to remove it from your active feed. Dismissed notifications no longer appear in the dropdown. This action only affects your own feed — other users who received the same notification are unaffected.

**Dismiss all notifications**

Use the "Dismiss all" option to clear your entire active notification feed at once. All notifications in the current workspace — read and unread — are dismissed from your view.

> **Note:** Your notification state is personal. Marking a notification as read or dismissing it only affects what you see. Other users who received the same notification will still see it as unread in their own feed until they act on it.

---

## Navigating from a Notification

Some notifications include an action route — a direct link to the relevant page in Graphium. Clicking the notification body (not the dismiss control) will navigate you to that page:

- **Connection health notifications** navigate to the Connections page for the affected vault.
- **Playbook run notifications** navigate to the Playbooks page for the affected vault.
- **Membership notifications** (workspace or vault role/status changes) do not include a navigation route in V1.
