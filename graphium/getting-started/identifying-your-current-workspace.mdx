## Overview

The workspace indicator is the persistent element in the top-left of the navigation bar that tells you which workspace you are currently working in. It is always visible no matter where you are in Graphium AI — on the dashboard, inside a vault, or anywhere else in the application.

---

## The Workspace Indicator

The workspace indicator appears in the **top-left corner of the navigation bar**, immediately to the right of the Graphium logo. It consists of:

- A **colored square icon** displaying the workspace's two-letter abbreviation (e.g., **UA** for Urban Anesthesia)
- The **full workspace name** in white text
- A **chevron arrow (∨)** indicating the name is clickable
- A **gear icon (⚙)** to the right for accessing workspace settings

**The color of the icon is unique to each workspace**, making it easy to identify your active workspace at a glance — even before reading the name.

---

## Reading the Indicator

To confirm which workspace you are in, look at the top-left of the navigation bar at any time. For example:

| Indicator appearance | Active workspace |
|----------------------|-----------------|
| Teal **UA** icon + "Urban Anesthesia" | Urban Anesthesia |
| Purple **AR** icon + "Apex Radiology" | Apex Radiology |
| Orange **GC** icon + "Graphium Corporate" | Graphium Corporate |
| Purple **AA** icon + "Allied Anesthesia" | Allied Anesthesia |

---

## Active Workspace in the Dropdown

When you click the workspace indicator to open the switcher dropdown, your **currently active workspace appears highlighted** at the top of the list — shown with a distinct background and colored text. All other workspaces in the list appear in standard black text without a highlight.

This highlighted state makes it easy to confirm your current workspace at a glance, even while reviewing what other workspaces are available.

---

## Where the Indicator Appears

The workspace indicator is visible from every page in Graphium AI, including:

- The **dashboard** (vault list view)
- Inside any **vault**, across all tables, views, and settings
- Within **workflow**, **automation**, and **AI assistant** views
- Any other page reachable from the navigation bar

The indicator does not disappear or change appearance based on what you are doing — it always reflects your active workspace.

---

## Who Can See the Indicator

All users can see the workspace indicator regardless of role. It is visible as soon as you are signed in and remains displayed throughout your session.

---

## Troubleshooting

**The workspace name in the indicator is not the one I expected.**
You may be in a different workspace than intended. Click the indicator to open the switcher and select the correct workspace.

**The indicator shows a workspace I don't recognize.**
Your account may have been added to an additional workspace by an administrator. Contact your workspace administrator if you have questions about your workspace access.

**The indicator is not visible.**
Try refreshing the page. If the navigation bar does not load, check your internet connection.
