## Overview

The workspace indicator is the persistent element in the top-left of the navigation bar that tells you which workspace you are currently working in. It is always visible no matter where you are in Graphium AI — on the dashboard, inside a vault, or anywhere else in the application.

---

## The Workspace Indicator

The workspace indicator appears in the **top-left corner of the navigation bar**, immediately to the right of the Graphium logo. It consists of:

- A **colored square icon** displaying the workspace's two-letter abbreviation (e.g., **UA** for Urban Anesthesia)
- The **full workspace name** in white text
- A **chevron arrow (∨)** indicating the name is clickable
- A **gear icon (⚙)** to the right for accessing workspace settings

**The color of the icon is unique to each workspace**, making it easy to identify your active workspace at a glance — even before reading the name.

## Active Workspace in the Dropdown

When you click the workspace indicator to open the switcher dropdown, your **currently active workspace appears highlighted** at the top of the list — shown with a distinct background and colored text. All other workspaces in the list appear in standard black text without a highlight.

This highlighted state makes it easy to confirm your current workspace at a glance, even while reviewing what other workspaces are available.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Workspace name in the indicator is not the one I expected | You may be in a different workspace than intended | Click the indicator to open the switcher and select the correct workspace |
| Indicator shows a workspace I don't recognize | Account was added to an additional workspace by an administrator | Contact your workspace administrator if you have questions about your workspace access |
| Indicator is not visible | Navigation bar failed to load | Refresh the page; check your internet connection |
