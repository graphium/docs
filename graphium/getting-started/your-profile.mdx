## Overview

Your profile is accessible from the top-right corner of the navigation bar on every page in Graphium AI. The profile block shows your display name and email address and serves as the entry point for viewing your account details, editing your information, and logging out.

---

## Viewing Your Profile

1. Look to the top-right corner of the navigation bar. You will see a circular avatar showing your initials (e.g., **MS** for Molly Sample) followed by your display name and a dropdown arrow.
2. Click your name or avatar to open the profile block.
3. The profile block drops down displaying your display name in bold and your email address beneath it.

## Editing Your Profile

1. Click your **name or avatar** in the top-right corner of the navigation bar to open the profile block.
2. Click **Edit Profile**. The profile editing view opens.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/edit-profile.png" alt="Graphium AI edit profile" />
</Frame>

3. Update the **Username**, **Email**, or both fields as needed.
4. Click **Save**. Your changes are reflected in the navigation bar and profile block immediately.

The Edit Profile form contains two fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Username** | Your display name as shown in the navigation bar and profile block |
| **Email** | The email address associated with your account, used for sign-in and notifications |

> **Note:** If you change your email address, you may be required to verify the new address before it becomes active, depending on your organization's authentication configuration.

> **Note:** If the save request fails, an error message will appear. Your existing profile information remains unchanged until a successful save is completed.

## Permissions

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Name or email shown is incorrect | Profile reflects account data stored with Graphium AI | Click **Edit Profile** within the profile block, or contact your workspace administrator |
| Profile block shows a loading state or no information | Connectivity or loading issue | Refresh the page and open the profile block again |
| Edit Profile option is not visible in the dropdown | Profile dropdown may not have fully loaded | Refresh the page and try opening the dropdown again |
| Changes are not saving | Network issue or invalid field value | Check your internet connection and ensure all fields are valid, then try again |
| Updated name is not reflected in the navigation bar | Browser cache or display delay | Refresh the page; your updated name should appear after a successful save |
| Email change is not taking effect | New email may require verification | Check your inbox for a verification email and follow the link to confirm |
| Save button does nothing | Browser compatibility issue | Try in a different browser or incognito/private window |
