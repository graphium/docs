# Requesting a Password Reset

**Linear Issue:** AI-2 (Request password reset via forgot password flow)
**Category:** Getting Started
**Roles Required:** None (public — accessible without signing in)

---

## Overview

If you've forgotten your Graphium AI password, you can request a reset link by email directly from the sign-in page. The process involves two phases: requesting the reset email, and then using that email to set a new password. This flow is only available for accounts registered with an email and password — if you signed up using Google, sign in with Google instead.

---

## Before You Begin

- Your Graphium AI account must have been created using an **email address and password** (not Google OAuth)
- You must have access to the email inbox associated with your account
- Password reset emails come from Graphium AI — check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes

---

## Phase 1: Request the Reset Email

1. Navigate to the Graphium AI sign-in page at [graphium.ai](https://graphium.ai).

2. Click **Forgot password?** — this link appears to the right of the **Remember me** checkbox, just above the **Sign in** button.

3. On the Forgot Password page, enter the **email address** associated with your Graphium AI account.

4. Click **Send**.

5. If the email address matches an existing account, a password reset email will be sent to your inbox. The email contains a **Reset my Password** button.

> **Note:** For security reasons, Graphium AI does not confirm whether a specific email address has an account. If you don't receive an email, double-check that you entered the correct address.

---

## Phase 2: Set Your New Password

1. Open the password reset email in your inbox and click **Reset my Password**.

2. You will be taken to a password reset page. Confirm your **email address** in the field provided.

3. Enter your **new password** in the password field.

4. Click **Reset my Password**.

5. Once the reset is complete, you can sign in using your email address and new password.

---

## After Resetting Your Password

- Your old password is immediately invalidated — any active sessions using the old password will be signed out
- You can sign in right away using your new credentials
- If you were attempting to accept a workspace invitation, you can return to the invitation link after signing in

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| No reset email received | Wrong email entered, or email routed to spam | Check your spam/junk folder; re-attempt using the exact email you registered with |
| Reset link doesn't work | Link has expired or already been used | Return to the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**, and request a new link |
| "No account found" message | Email is not registered with Graphium AI | Try a different email address, or register a new account |
| Signed up with Google | Password reset does not apply to Google accounts | Return to the sign-in page and click **Continue with Google** instead |
| Still can't access your account | Persistent issue | Contact your workspace admin or Graphium AI support |
