# Signing In with Email and Password

**Linear Issue:** AI-1 (Sign in to account with email and password)
**Category:** Getting Started
**Roles Required:** None (public — accessible without an existing session)

---

## Overview

To access Graphium AI, navigate to the sign-in page and enter your registered email address and password. After successful authentication, your workspace loads automatically and you are taken to the dashboard. This article covers the email and password sign-in method.

> **Note:** If your organization uses Google to authenticate, sign in using **Continue with Google** instead. See [Getting Started — Signing In with Google] for that flow.

---

## Before You Begin

- You must have a Graphium AI account. If you don't have one yet, see [Registering a New Account](/graphium/getting-started/registering-a-new-account)
- Have your registered **email address** and **password** ready
- Sign-in is available from the Graphium AI web dashboard — use a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)

---

## How to Sign In

1. Navigate to the Graphium AI sign-in page at [graphium.ai](https://graphium.ai).

   The sign-in screen displays with a **"Welcome back"** heading and the message *"Sign in to your account to continue."*

2. Enter your registered **email address** in the Email field.

3. Enter your **password** in the Password field.

4. Optionally, check **Remember me** if you are on a trusted personal device and want to stay signed in across browser sessions.

5. Click **Sign in**.

---

## What Happens After Sign-In

- Graphium AI authenticates your credentials and issues a secure session
- Your account and workspace context loads automatically in the background
- You are taken to the **Dashboard** — your home for vaults, workflows, tasks, and notifications
- Your session is maintained with a secure, short-lived access token. You will be prompted to sign in again if your session expires or you sign out

> **Tip:** If you have been sent a workspace invitation link, clicking that link before signing in will automatically accept the invitation and add you to the workspace when you authenticate — no extra steps required.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| "Invalid email or password" error | Incorrect credentials entered | Double-check for typos; ensure Caps Lock is off; try resetting your password via **Forgot password?** |
| Forgot your password | — | Click **Forgot password?** to the right of the Remember me checkbox. See [Requesting a Password Reset](/graphium/getting-started/requesting-a-password-reset) |
| Account not found | Email address is not registered | Check you are using the correct email; contact your workspace admin if you believe you have an account |
| Signed up with Google | Email/password sign-in does not apply | Return to the sign-in page and use **Continue with Google** instead |
| Sign-in button does nothing | Browser extension or compatibility issue | Try in an incognito/private window or a different browser |
| Page won't load | Network or firewall issue | Check your internet connection; contact IT if on a VPN |
| Workspace is missing after sign-in | Account exists but was not added to the workspace | Contact your workspace admin to send a new invitation |
