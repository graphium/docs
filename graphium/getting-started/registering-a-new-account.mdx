## Overview

To use Graphium AI, you first need to create an account. Registration takes just a few minutes and sets up both your personal account and your first workspace in one step. You can register using an email address and password, or sign up using your Google account.

> **Note:** Registration is currently only available from the Graphium AI web dashboard.

## Before You Begin

- You will need a valid business email address (or a Google account)
- Have your business name ready — this becomes both your **Customer name** and your first **Workspace name**, and can be updated later
- You must agree to Graphium AI's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy to complete registration

---

## Option A: Register with Email and Password

1. Navigate to the Graphium AI sign-in page at [app.graphium.ai/sign-in](https://app.graphium.ai/sign-in).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/sign-in-page.png" alt="Graphium AI sign-in page" />
</Frame>

2. Click the **Sign up** link at the bottom of the sign-in form ("Don't have an account? **Sign up**").

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/sign-up-page.png" alt="Graphium AI sign-up page" />
</Frame>

3. On the registration form, fill in the following fields:

   | **First Name** | Your first name |
   | **Last Name** | Your last name |
   | **Business Name** | Used as your Customer and Workspace name |
   | **Email Address** | Your business email — used to sign in |
   | **Password** | Choose a strong password |

4. Check the box to agree to the **Terms of Service and Privacy Policy**.

5. Click **Start my Trial**.

6. You will be signed in automatically and taken to your new Graphium AI workspace dashboard.

---

## Option B: Register with Google

1. Navigate to the Graphium AI sign-in page at [app.graphium.ai/sign-in](https://app.graphium.ai/sign-in).

2. Click the **Sign up** link at the bottom of the sign-in form.

3. On the registration page, click **Sign up with Google**.

4. A Google authentication popup will appear. Sign in to your Google account and grant the requested permissions.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/google-auth-popup.png" alt="Google authentication popup" />
</Frame>

5. After a successful Google login, you will be prompted to fill in your business details (Business Name, and any remaining fields not pre-filled from your Google profile).

6. Check the box to agree to the **Terms of Service and Privacy Policy**.

7. Click **Start my Trial**.

8. You will be signed in and taken to your new Graphium AI workspace dashboard.

---

## What Happens After Registration

- Your **account** is created, linked to the email address you registered with
- Your first **workspace** is created automatically using your Business Name
- You are set as the **ADMIN** of your new workspace
- You are signed in immediately — no separate email verification step is required before accessing the dashboard
- The workspace is ready for you to create vaults, invite team members, and start capturing data

## Registering via an Invitation Link

If a colleague has invited you to join their workspace, you may receive an invitation link by email. Clicking that link before signing up will:

- Pre-fill the registration flow with the invitation context
- Automatically add you as a member of their workspace once your account is created
- Sign you into the invited workspace rather than creating a new one of your own

Follow the same registration steps above — the invitation is accepted automatically when you complete sign-up.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| "Email already in use" error | An account already exists with that email | Click **Sign in** instead, or use **Forgot password?** to regain access |
| Google popup is blocked | Browser pop-up blocker is active | Allow pop-ups for app.graphium.ai/sign-in and try again |
| "Invalid invitation" error | The invitation link has expired or already been used | Ask the workspace admin to resend the invitation |
| Registration button is greyed out | Not all required fields are filled, or Terms checkbox is unchecked | Complete all required fields and check the Terms box |
| Not redirected to dashboard after sign-up | Browser or network issue | Refresh the page — if you are already signed in, you will be taken to the dashboard automatically |
