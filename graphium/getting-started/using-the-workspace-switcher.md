# Using the Workspace Switcher

**Linear issues:** AI-137, AI-139
**Linear project:** Workspace: Workspace Management
**Article type:** How-to

---

## Overview

The workspace switcher is the control in the top-left of the navigation bar that shows your currently active workspace and lets you move between any workspaces your account belongs to. You can open it and switch workspaces from any page in Graphium AI.

---

## Where to Find the Workspace Switcher

The workspace switcher is always visible in the **top-left corner of the navigation bar**, to the right of the Graphium logo. It displays:

- A **colored square icon** showing the workspace's two-letter abbreviation (e.g., **UA** for Urban Anesthesia)
- The **full name** of your currently active workspace
- A **chevron arrow (∨)** indicating it is a clickable dropdown
- A **gear icon (⚙)** to the right for workspace settings

**Example:** A workspace named "Apex Radiology" appears as a purple **AR** icon followed by **Apex Radiology ∨**.

---

## How to Open the Workspace Switcher

1. From any page in Graphium AI, locate the workspace indicator in the **top-left of the navigation bar**.

2. Click the **workspace name** or the **chevron arrow (∨)** next to it. The workspace switcher dropdown opens immediately below.

3. The dropdown lists all workspaces available to your account. Your currently active workspace appears highlighted at the top of the list, with each workspace shown as a colored icon and full name.

4. To close the dropdown without making a change, press **Escape** or click anywhere outside it.

---

## What the Dropdown Shows

When open, the workspace switcher lists each workspace with a colored square icon (two-letter abbreviation) and the full workspace name. The currently active workspace is highlighted. At the bottom of the list, a **"Manage workspaces"** button (with a gear icon) links to the full workspace management page. For example:

| Icon | Workspace Name | Status |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **UA** (teal) | Urban Anesthesia | Active (highlighted) |
| **AR** (purple) | Apex Radiology | — |
| **GC** (orange) | Graphium Corporate | — |
| **AA** (purple) | Allied Anesthesia | — |
| ⚙ | Manage workspaces | — |

---

## How to Switch to a Different Workspace

1. Open the workspace switcher by clicking the workspace name or chevron arrow (∨) in the top-left nav bar.

2. Click the name of the workspace you want to switch to.

3. The dropdown closes and the page refreshes. The nav bar updates to show the new workspace's colored icon and name, and the dashboard reloads to display that workspace's vaults.

---

## What Changes When You Switch

When you switch workspaces, the following updates immediately:

- The **workspace indicator** in the top-left nav bar changes to the new workspace's icon and name
- The **dashboard** reloads and shows only the vaults belonging to the new workspace
- If the new workspace has no vaults, an empty state appears: *"No vaults yet — Create your first vault to start organizing your structured data, forms, and workflows."*
- All navigation, vaults, tables, and content reflect the new workspace

Your account, session, and profile information remain unchanged across workspaces.

---

## What Does Not Change

Switching workspaces does **not**:

- Log you out or affect your session
- Delete or modify any data in the previous workspace
- Change your user role or permissions (roles are set per workspace)
- Affect other users currently working in either workspace

---

## Example

If you are in **Urban Anesthesia** (teal **UA** icon) and want to move to **Apex Radiology**:

1. Click **Urban Anesthesia ∨** in the top-left nav bar
2. Click **Apex Radiology** in the dropdown
3. The nav bar updates to show a purple **AR** icon and "Apex Radiology"
4. The dashboard now shows Apex Radiology's vaults, or the empty state if none exist yet

---

## Who Can Switch Workspaces

The workspace switcher is accessible from every page in Graphium AI. Any user whose account belongs to more than one workspace can switch between them — no special role is required.

> **Note:** Administrative actions such as creating or deactivating workspaces are restricted to users with the **Workspace Owner** role.

---

## Troubleshooting

**The workspace switcher does not respond when clicked.**
Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, check your internet connection.

**The dropdown opened but closed immediately.**
This can happen if you accidentally clicked outside the dropdown. Click the workspace name again to reopen it.

**I only see one workspace in the dropdown.**
Your account may belong to only one workspace. Contact your workspace administrator if you believe you should have access to additional workspaces.

**The workspace I want to switch to is not in the list.**
Your account must be added to a workspace before it appears in the switcher. Contact your administrator to request access.

**After switching, the dashboard shows "No vaults yet."**
The selected workspace has no vaults. This is expected for a newly created or empty workspace. You can create a vault directly from this screen.

**The page did not update after I clicked a workspace.**
Refresh the page manually. If the workspace switcher still shows the old workspace, check your internet connection and try again.

**I accidentally switched to the wrong workspace.**
Open the workspace switcher again and click the correct workspace to switch back immediately.
