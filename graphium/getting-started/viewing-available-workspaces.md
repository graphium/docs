# Viewing Available Workspaces

**Linear issue:** AI-138
**Linear project:** Workspace: Workspace Management
**Article type:** How-to

---

## Overview

A workspace in Graphium AI is the top-level organizational container for your vaults, data, and team members. If your account belongs to multiple workspaces — for example, separate workspaces for different practices, facilities, or customer organizations — you can view and switch between them at any time from the navigation bar.

---

## Where to Find Your Workspaces

Your active workspace is always visible in the **top-left corner of the navigation bar**, displayed as a colored square icon with your workspace's two-letter abbreviation followed by the workspace name and a dropdown arrow.

**Example:** A workspace named "Urban Anesthesia" appears as a teal **UA** icon followed by the text **Urban Anesthesia ∨**.

---

## How to View Your Available Workspaces

1. From any page in Graphium AI, locate the **workspace indicator** in the top-left of the navigation bar. It shows your current workspace name next to a colored icon.

2. Click the workspace name (or the dropdown arrow **∨** beside it). A dropdown list opens showing all workspaces available to your account.

3. The list displays each workspace with:
   - A **colored square icon** showing the workspace's two-letter abbreviation
   - The **full workspace name**
   - The **currently active workspace** is highlighted in the list
   - A **"Manage workspaces"** button (with a gear icon) at the bottom of the list, which opens the full workspace management page

4. To switch to a different workspace, click its name in the list. The page will reload showing that workspace's vaults and data.

5. To close the dropdown without switching, press **Escape** or click anywhere outside the dropdown.

---

## What You Will See

When you open the workspace dropdown, each workspace in your account appears as a row in the list. For example, an account with multiple facilities might show:

- **UA** — Urban Anesthesia *(currently active, highlighted)*
- **AR** — Apex Radiology
- **GC** — Graphium Corporate
- **AA** — Allied Anesthesia

Each workspace icon uses a distinct color to help you identify workspaces at a glance. Your currently active workspace is indicated by highlighted text.

---

## Who Can View Workspaces

All users — regardless of role — can view the workspaces their account belongs to. Switching between workspaces requires only that your account has membership in that workspace.

> **Note:** Administrative actions such as creating or deactivating workspaces are restricted to users with the **Workspace Owner** role.

---

## Default Workspace

Your account may have a **default workspace** configured, which is the workspace you are automatically placed into when you sign in. The default workspace is set in your account settings by an administrator.

---

## The Manage Workspaces Page

Clicking **Manage workspaces** at the bottom of the switcher dropdown opens a dedicated workspace management page. This page gives you a full view of all workspaces your account belongs to and lets you switch between them.

The page displays:

- An **"Add a workspace"** button at the top (visible to Workspace Owners)
- A list of all your workspaces, each showing:
  - The workspace's colored icon and full name
  - **"You are currently in this workspace"** — shown on your active workspace in place of a switch button
  - **"Switch to this workspace"** button — shown on every other workspace; clicking it switches you into that workspace immediately

---

## Troubleshooting

**I only see one workspace.**
Your account may only belong to one workspace. If you believe you should have access to additional workspaces, contact your workspace administrator.

**The workspace I need is not in the list.**
You must be added to a workspace by a Workspace Owner before it appears in your list. Contact your administrator to request access.

**The workspace list is not loading.**
Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. The workspace list typically loads within two seconds.

**I switched workspaces but my vaults look different.**
Each workspace contains its own set of vaults and data. Switching workspaces shows only the vaults and content belonging to the workspace you selected.
