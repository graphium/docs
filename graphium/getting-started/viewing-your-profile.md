# Viewing Your Profile

Your profile information is accessible from the navigation bar at the top of every page in Graphium AI. The profile block shows your display name and email address and serves as the entry point for profile-related actions.

---

## How to View Your Profile

1. Look to the top-right corner of the navigation bar. You will see a circular avatar showing your initials (e.g., **DD** for Daniel Dura) followed by your display name and a dropdown arrow.
2. Click your name or avatar to open the profile block.
3. The profile block drops down and displays your identity information.

The profile block is available from every page in the application — the dashboard, inside any vault, and all other views.

---

## What the Profile Block Shows

When open, the profile block displays:

- **Your display name** — shown in bold at the top (e.g., Daniel Dura)
- **Your email address** — shown in smaller text beneath your name (e.g., daniel.dura@gmail.com)

This information reflects your current account as authenticated with Graphium AI and loads within a few seconds of opening the block.

---

## Actions Available from the Profile Block

In addition to your identity information, the profile block provides two actions:

- **Edit Profile** — opens the profile editing view to update your account details. See *Getting Started — Managing Your Profile* for more information.
- **Log Out** — ends your session and returns you to the sign-in page. See *Logging Out of Graphium AI* for more information.

---

## Who Can View Their Profile

All user roles can view their own profile information, including Workspace Owner, Workspace Manager, Form Editor, Form Viewer, and Integration Manager.

---

## Troubleshooting

**My name or email address shown is incorrect.**
The profile block reflects the account data stored with Graphium AI. If your information needs to be updated, click **Edit Profile** within the profile block, or contact your workspace administrator if you are unable to make changes yourself.

**The profile block shows a loading state or no information.**
Profile data typically loads within a few seconds. If it does not appear, try refreshing the page and opening the profile block again. If the problem persists, it may indicate a connectivity issue — check your network and try again.
