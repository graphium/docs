## Overview

Workspace Owners can invite new members to the workspace from the **Members & Invitations** page in Workspace Settings. An invitation email is sent to the address provided. The invitee's status shows as **Pending** in the Pending Invitations section until they accept.

---

## How to Send an Invite

1. In the top navigation bar, click the **Workspace Settings** button next to the workspace name.
2. Click **Members** in the left sidebar.
3. In the **Invite New User** section at the top of the page, enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/invite-workspace-user.png" alt="Invite workspace user" />
</Frame>

4. Select the role they should have from the role dropdown (defaults to **Member**).
5. Click **Send Invite**.

An invitation email is sent to the address provided. The invitee appears in the **Pending Invitations** section below the members table until they accept.

> **Note:** Only users who have not yet joined the workspace can be invited this way. To add an existing workspace member to a specific vault, see [Adding a User to a Vault](Adding%20a%20User%20to%20a%20Vault.md).

## What Happens After Inviting

- The invitee receives an email with a link to accept the invitation and create their account (if they don't have one).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/workspace-invitation-email.png" alt="Workspace invitation email" />
</Frame>

- Once accepted, they appear in the **Workspace Members** table with an **Active** status.
- The invitation can be resent or revoked at any time from the **Pending Invitations** section. See [Checking Workspace Invitation Status](Checking%20Workspace%20Invitation%20Status.md) for details.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Invite button is not visible | You may not have the Owner role | Only Workspace Owners can send invitations; contact your workspace admin |
| Invitee did not receive the email | Email routed to spam, or incorrect address entered | Ask the invitee to check their spam folder; resend or revoke the invitation and re-enter the correct address |
| Invitation shows as Pending but invitee can't find the email | Email delivery delay or filtered | Resend the invitation from the Pending Invitations section |
| "User already exists" or invitation fails to send | The email address is already associated with a workspace member | The user may already be a member; check the Workspace Members table |
| Invitation link expired | Invitation links expire after a set period | Revoke the old invitation and send a new one |
