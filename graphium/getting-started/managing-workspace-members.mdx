## Overview

The **Members & Invitations** page in Workspace Settings is where Workspace Owners manage everyone who has access to the workspace — sending invitations, monitoring invitation status, viewing active members, and deactivating or reactivating access. Only Workspace Owners can view and manage workspace members.

---

## Viewing Workspace Members

1. In the top navigation bar, click the **Workspace Settings** button next to the workspace name.
2. Click **Members** in the left sidebar.

The **Workspace Members** table lists every person who has accepted their invitation and joined the workspace. Each row shows:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **User** | The member's avatar, full name, and email address |
| **Role** | The member's workspace role — **Owner** or **Member** |
| **Status** | **Active** (green) or **Deactivated** (grey) |
| **Actions** | **Deactivate** for active members, or **Reactivate** for deactivated members |

## Inviting a User to the Workspace

1. From the **Members** page, go to the **Invite New User** section at the top.
2. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/invite-workspace-user.png" alt="Invite workspace user" />
</Frame>

3. Select their role from the dropdown (defaults to **Member**).
4. Click **Send Invite**.

An invitation email is sent to the address provided. The invitee appears in the **Pending Invitations** section until they accept.

> **Note:** Only users who have not yet joined the workspace can be invited this way.

**What Happens After Inviting:**

- The invitee receives an email with a link to accept the invitation and create their account (if they don't have one).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/workspace-invitation-email.png" alt="Workspace invitation email" />
</Frame>

- Once accepted, they appear in the **Workspace Members** table with an **Active** status.
- The invitation can be resent or revoked at any time from the **Pending Invitations** section.

## Checking Invitation Status

Invitations that have been sent but not yet accepted appear in the **Pending Invitations** section below the members table. Each row shows the invitee's email address, when the invite was sent, their assigned role, and a **Pending** status badge.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/graphium/getting-started/pending-workspace-invite.png" alt="Pending workspace invite" />
</Frame>

From this section, Workspace Owners can:

- Click **Resend invitation** to re-send the invitation email.
- Click **Revoke** to cancel the invitation immediately.

**Member Status Values:**

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Active** | The member has accepted their invitation and has an active account |
| **Pending** | An invitation has been sent but not yet accepted |
| **Deactivated** | The member's access has been suspended |

---

## Removing a Workspace Member

1. In the **Workspace Members** table, locate the member you want to deactivate.
2. Click the **Deactivate** link in the **Actions** column.
3. Confirm by clicking **Deactivate** in the confirmation dialog.

The member's status immediately changes to **Deactivated** and they lose access to the workspace. Their data and records remain intact — deactivation only affects access.

To restore access, click **Reactivate** in their Actions column. Reactivation takes effect immediately.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Invite button is not visible | You may not have the Owner role | Only Workspace Owners can send invitations; contact your workspace admin |
| Invitee did not receive the email | Email routed to spam, or incorrect address entered | Ask the invitee to check their spam folder; resend or re-enter the correct address |
| Invitation shows as Pending but invitee can't find the email | Email delivery delay or filtered | Resend the invitation from the Pending Invitations section |
| Invitee accepted but does not appear in Workspace Members | Page may not have refreshed | Refresh the page and check again |
| Cannot see the Members page | You may not have the Owner role | Only Workspace Owners can view and manage members |
| Invitation link expired | Invitation links expire after a set period | Revoke the old invitation and send a new one |
