## Overview

The dashboard is your home screen in Graphium AI. It displays all vaults in your workspace as either a grid of cards or a list of rows. From here you can browse your vaults and open any one to access its tables, tasks, automations, and settings.

---

## The Vault Dashboard

By default, vaults are displayed as cards in a responsive grid. Each card gives you a quick summary before you open the vault:

- **Vault icon** — displayed in the upper-left corner using the icon and color assigned to that vault
- **Vault name** — shown in bold below the icon
- **Vault description** — a brief description beneath the name; long descriptions are truncated
- **Record count** — total number of records across all tables in the vault
- **Table count** — total number of tables in the vault

The first card in the grid is always the **Create a Vault** card (dashed border, **+** icon). Clicking it opens the vault creation flow.

---

## Switching Between Grid and List View

Use the view toggle icons in the upper-right corner of the dashboard:

- **Grid icon (⊞)** — displays vaults as cards with names, record counts, and table counts
- **List icon (☰)** — displays vaults as rows with Name, Last Updated, Records and Tables columns

Your view preference is remembered across sessions.

---

## Opening a Vault

Click anywhere on a vault card or row to open it. The vault opens landing you on its first table in the **Data** view.

Each time you update a vault, the **Last Updated** timestamp updates automatically. Only vaults you have been granted access to appear on your dashboard.

---

## What You See When a Vault Opens

When you open a vault you are taken to the vault interior, which includes:

- A **vault header** at the top showing the vault name, record count, and last updated date
- A **sidebar** on the left with AI Assistant, your list of tables, Tasks, Automations, and Settings
- The **first table's Data view** as the default landing point

To return to the dashboard, click the **back arrow (←)** in the top-left corner of the vault header.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| A vault is missing from the dashboard | You haven't been added to that vault, or it is inactive | Contact your workspace administrator to request access |
| The grid shows fewer vaults than expected | You may be in the wrong workspace | Check the workspace name in the top navigation bar |
| Clicking a vault card does nothing | A temporary UI issue | Refresh the page and try again |
| The vault opens on an unexpected table | The first table in the vault's list is shown by default | Use the sidebar to navigate to the table you want |
| The dashboard shows "No vaults yet" | No vaults have been created in this workspace | Use the **+ Create Vault** card to create one, or ask an Admin |
