# Opening a Vault from the Dashboard

**Linear Issue:** AI-6 (Open an existing vault from the dashboard)
**Category:** Vaults
**Roles Required:** All roles (ADMIN, BUILDER, CONTRIBUTOR, VIEWER)

---

## Overview

The dashboard is your home screen in Graphium AI. It shows all the vaults in your workspace as either a grid of cards or a list of rows. Opening a vault takes you inside it, where you can access its tables, tasks, automations, and settings.

---

## Opening a Vault in Grid View

When the dashboard is in **grid view** (the default), each vault appears as a card showing its name, description, record count, and last updated date.

1. From the dashboard, locate the vault you want to open.
2. Click anywhere on the vault card.
3. The vault opens, landing you on its first table in the **Data** view.

> **Tip:** When you hover over a vault card, a quick-open icon appears in the top-right corner of the card. Clicking that icon also opens the vault.

---

## Opening a Vault in List View

When the dashboard is in **list view**, vaults appear as rows in a table with **Name**, **Last Opened**, and **Records** columns.

1. From the dashboard, click the **list view** icon (☰) in the upper-right corner to switch to list view if you are not already there.
2. Locate the vault row you want to open.
3. Click anywhere on the row.
4. The vault opens, landing you on its first table in the **Data** view.

---

## Switching Between Grid and List View

Use the view toggle icons in the upper-right corner of the dashboard to switch between views:

- **Grid icon (⊞)** — Displays vaults as cards with names, descriptions, and metadata
- **List icon (☰)** — Displays vaults as rows with Name, Last Opened, and Records columns

Your view preference is remembered across sessions.

---

## What You See When a Vault Opens

When you open a vault, you are taken to the vault interior, which includes:

- A **vault header** at the top showing the vault name, record count, and last updated date
- A **sidebar** on the left with AI Assistant, your list of tables, Tasks, Automations, and Settings
- The **first table's Data view** as the default landing point

The vault header shows:

- **Record count** — The total number of records across all tables in the vault
- **Last updated** — The date the vault was most recently modified

---

## Returning to the Dashboard

To go back to the dashboard from inside a vault:

1. Click the **back arrow (←)** in the top-left corner of the vault header, to the left of the vault icon.
2. The dashboard reappears, showing your full vault list.

---

## Vault Access and the "Last Opened" Timestamp

Each time you open a vault, the **Last Opened** time shown in list view updates automatically. This reflects when you most recently accessed the vault and displays as relative time — for example, "Opened 1 minutes ago" or "Opened 2 days ago."

Only vaults you have been granted access to appear on your dashboard. If you cannot see a vault you expect to find, contact your workspace administrator.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | What to Do |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| A vault is missing from the dashboard | You have not been added to that vault, or the vault is inactive | Contact your workspace administrator to request access |
| Clicking a vault card does nothing | A temporary UI issue | Refresh the page and try again |
| The vault opens on an unexpected table | The first table in the vault's list is shown by default | Use the sidebar to navigate to the table you want |
| The dashboard shows "No vaults yet" | No vaults have been created in your workspace yet | Use the **+ Create Vault** button to create one, or ask an ADMIN to create one |
