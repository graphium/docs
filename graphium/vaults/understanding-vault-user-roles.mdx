## Overview

Every user with vault access is assigned one of four roles. The role controls what that person can see and do inside the vault. Roles are displayed in the **Access & Permissions → Users** tab and can be changed by a vault Admin at any time.

---

## The Four Vault Roles

| Role | What They Can Do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access to manage the vault, its users, and all data. Can change vault settings, add and remove users, change roles, create and modify tables, forms, and workflows, and add and edit records. |
| **Builder** | Can create and modify tables, forms, and workflows. Can add and edit records but cannot manage vault users or settings. |
| **Contributor** | Can add and edit data records but cannot modify vault structure (tables, forms, workflows). Cannot access vault settings or user management. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access. Can view records and data but cannot add, edit, or delete anything. |

---

## Where to See a User's Role

A user's current vault role is visible on every row of the **Access & Permissions → Users** tab. The role appears as a dropdown selector on the right side of each user row.

To navigate there:
1. Open the vault and click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Access & Permissions**.
3. Each user row displays their current role in the role dropdown.

---

## Changing a Role

Only vault **Admins** can change a user's role. See [Changing a Users Vault Role](Changing%20a%20Users%20Vault%20Role.md) for instructions.

---

## Permissions

| Role | Can See Their Own Role |
|------|------------------------|
| Admin | ✅ (via Access & Permissions page) |
| Builder | ✅ (via Access & Permissions page) |
| Contributor | ❌ (no Settings access) |
| Viewer | ❌ (no Settings access) |

> **Note:** All users can observe what their role allows them to do — for example, a Contributor will notice they cannot access table settings. But only Admins and Builders can view the Access & Permissions page where roles are explicitly listed.
