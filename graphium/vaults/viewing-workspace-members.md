# Viewing Workspace Members

**Linear issue:** AI-576
**Linear project:** Vault/Workspace Settings: User Permissions
**Article type:** Reference

---

## Overview

The **Members & Invitations** page in Workspace Settings shows every person who has been granted access to your workspace, along with their role and account status. Pending invitations are shown in a separate section below the members list. Only **Workspace Owners** can view and manage workspace members.

---

## How to Open the Members List

1. In the top navigation bar, click the **Workspace Settings** button next to the workspace name.
2. Click **Members** in the left sidebar.

The **Members & Invitations** page opens.

---

## What You See in the Members List

The **Workspace Members** table lists every person who has accepted their invitation and joined the workspace. Each row represents one member.

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **User** | The member's avatar (initials circle or person icon), full name, and email address |
| **Role** | The member's workspace role — **Owner** or **Member** |
| **Status** | Whether the account is active — **Active** (green badge) or **Deactivated** (grey badge) |
| **Actions** | **Deactivate** (red link) for active members, or **Reactivate** (green link) for deactivated members |

---

## Pending Invitations

Workspace members who have not yet accepted their invitation are shown in a separate **Pending Invitations** section below the members table. Each pending invitation row shows the invitee's email address, the time elapsed since the invite was sent, their assigned role, and a **Pending** status badge.

From the Pending Invitations section you can:

- Click **Resend invitation** to re-send the invitation email.
- Click **Revoke** to cancel the invitation.

See [Checking Workspace Invitation Status](Checking%20Workspace%20Invitation%20Status.md) for more details.

---

## Role Permissions

Each workspace member is assigned one of the following roles:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to manage the workspace, members, and settings |
| **Member** | Standard access to vaults and workspace features |

---

## Permissions

| Role | Can View Workspace Members |
|------|--------------------------|
| Owner | ✅ |
| Member | ❌ |
