## Overview

Vault Admins can change another user's vault role at any time from the **Access & Permissions → Users** tab. The role change takes effect immediately — no confirmation is required and no notification is sent to the affected user.

---

## Steps

1. Open the vault and click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Access & Permissions**.
3. In the **Users** tab, locate the user whose role you want to change.
4. Click the **role dropdown** on the right side of their row. It currently shows their assigned role (Admin, Builder, Contributor, or Viewer).
5. Select the new role from the dropdown.

The role updates immediately. The user's access changes the next time they interact with the vault.

---

## Available Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access to manage vault, users, and all data |
| **Builder** | Can create and modify tables, forms, and workflows |
| **Contributor** | Can add and edit data, but cannot modify structure |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to view data |

---

## Things to Know

- Role changes take effect immediately — no save button is needed.
- You can also change the role for a **Pending** user (one who has not yet accepted their invitation). The role set now will be their role when they accept.
- A vault must always have at least one Admin. If you are the only Admin, you cannot change your own role to a lower level — doing so would leave the vault unmanaged.
