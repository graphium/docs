## Overview

The vault sidebar width is adjustable. A drag handle sits on the right edge of the sidebar — clicking and dragging it lets you widen or narrow the sidebar to suit your preference. This is useful if your table names are long and get truncated, or if you want to maximize the space available for the main content area.

---

## Resizing the Sidebar

**Steps:**

1. Open a vault. The sidebar is visible on the left side of the screen.
2. Hover your cursor over the right edge of the sidebar. The cursor changes to a horizontal resize cursor (↔) to indicate the drag handle is active.
3. Click and drag the edge to the left to narrow the sidebar, or to the right to widen it.
4. Release the mouse button when the sidebar is the width you want.

The new width is applied immediately. Table names, section labels, and navigation items reflow to fit the adjusted width.

---

## Collapsing vs. Resizing

Resizing adjusts the sidebar to a custom width while keeping all text labels visible. Collapsing the sidebar is a separate action that hides all text labels entirely, reducing the sidebar to a narrow icon-only bar. For details on collapsing and expanding, see [Navigating Your Vault Using the Sidebar](/graphium/navigation/navigating-your-vault-using-the-sidebar).

---

## Permissions

All vault member roles — **ADMIN**, **BUILDER**, **CONTRIBUTOR**, and **VIEWER** — can resize the sidebar. The width preference is local to the current session.
