## Overview

The **Billing** page gives workspace Owners and Admins a single place to review the workspace's current subscription state — the active plan, billing period, seat counts, Graphium Credits balance, and the resource limits that apply to the workspace. Only users with a workspace **Owner** or **Admin** role can access the Billing page.

---

## Opening the Billing Page

From anywhere in the application, click the **gear icon** (⚙️) in the top navigation bar to open workspace **Settings**. In the Settings sidebar, click **Billing**.

If you do not see a Billing option in Settings, your workspace role does not include billing access. Contact your workspace Owner or Admin.

---

## Current Plan and Subscription Status

The top section of the Billing page displays your workspace's active subscription:

- **Plan name** — the name of the plan your workspace is currently on
- **Subscription status** — whether the subscription is active, in a trial period, or pending a change
- **Billing period** — the current billing cycle start and end dates

If the workspace has not yet started a paid subscription, this section explains the current free or trial state and provides an entry point to review available plans.

---

## Seat Composition

The Billing page shows the number of **Builder** and **Contributor** billing seats currently purchased for the workspace, along with how many are assigned to members. For more information on seat types and how to manage them, see [Managing Billing Seats](/graphium/billing/managing-billing-seats).

---

## Graphium Credits

Graphium Credits are used to power AI features within the workspace, such as AI Assistant conversations and AI-driven playbook actions. The Credits section of the Billing page shows:

- **Included credits** — credits bundled with the current plan that reset each billing period
- **Purchased credits** — additional credits added on top of the plan's included balance
- **Remaining balance** — a summary of total credits available and usage to date in the current period

Credits are shared across the workspace and are consumed by all vault activity that uses AI features.

---

## Plan Limits and Entitlements

Every subscription plan includes a set of workspace-level limits. The Billing page displays the current entitlement values for your plan, including:

- Maximum number of vaults in the workspace
- Maximum custom tables per vault
- Maximum records per vault
- Maximum active playbooks per vault
- Maximum custom AI assistants per vault
- Maximum connections per vault
- Other limits defined by your current plan

These values reflect what your workspace is entitled to based on the active subscription. If the workspace approaches or reaches a limit, Graphium displays an in-app prompt guiding you to the Billing page to review upgrade options. See [Plan Limits and Upgrade Guidance](/graphium/billing/subscription-and-payment-settings#plan-limits-and-upgrade-guidance) for more detail on how limit enforcement works.

---

## Payment and Invoice Management

The right side of the Billing page includes a **Billing portal** card with an **Open billing portal** button. This opens your workspace's billing portal (managed by Stripe) where you can update your saved payment method, manage subscription settings, and download receipts.

A **Recent Invoices** section also appears on the right side of the page, showing the most recent charges. Click **View all invoices** to open the full invoice history in the billing portal.

Both of these routes to the billing portal return you to Graphium once you are done.
